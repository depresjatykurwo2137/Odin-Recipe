Beef Stroganoff Recipe

Description

This classic Beef Stroganoff recipe is a hearty dish packed with tender beef and a creamy mushroom sauce. Serve it over egg noodles for a satisfying dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin steak
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the beef until browned. Remove from skillet.
  2. In the same skillet, cook the onions, garlic, and mushrooms until tender.
  3. Add the beef broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until the broth has reduced by half.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the sour cream and flour. Stir into the skillet and cook until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Add the beef back into the skillet and heat through. Season with salt and pepper.
